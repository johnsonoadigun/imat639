Our organization and training programs were born out of the desire to help people living with disabilities experience life to the fullest and get a jump start on the path to independence.

As this organization grows, we want to continue to be mentors to this population. The most effective way to make his organization accessible to our targeted population is through a multimedia website, hence the creation of this website.

Our goal as an organization is to help people with special needs reach their highest potential of independence with the least possible cost to them.

We are levaraging power of techonology and internet advertising to reach as many interested mentors as and as many students who can benefit from our programs.
